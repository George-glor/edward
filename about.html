About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to quality auto repair in Torrance, CA.

Our Story

Edward's Collision Center was founded in 1998 by Edward Glor, a certified auto body technician with over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry. What started as a small shop has grown into one of Torrance's most trusted collision repair centers, known for our quality workmanship and customer service.

We've built our reputation on honesty, integrity, and exceptional service. Our team of ASE-certified technicians is committed to restoring your vehicle to its pre-accident condition using the latest technology and techniques.

At Edward's Collision Center, we understand that your vehicle is more than just a mode of transportation - it's an investment and a part of your daily life. That's why we treat every vehicle as if it were our own, with the utmost care and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional collision repair and auto body services through skilled craftsmanship, advanced technology, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. We strive to restore your vehicle to its pre-accident condition while ensuring your complete peace of mind throughout the process.
